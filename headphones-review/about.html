About Hz Club

Where brutalist design meets brutal honesty.

The Mission

Hz Club exists because most headphone reviews are boring. They're either too technical (here's a frequency response graph!) or too vague (these sound "musical"). We're different.

We review headphones like film critics review movies — with context, personality, and the occasional pretentious reference. Because your ears deserve better than "good bass response."

Our Philosophy

01

Specs don't lie. But they whisper.

Numbers matter, but they're not the whole story. A headphone can measure perfectly and still sound lifeless.

02

Price ≠ Quality

Some $30 headphones embarrass $300 ones. We'll tell you which ones.

03

Context is everything

Studio headphones for the subway? Wireless buds for critical listening? We'll save you from yourself.

Frequently Asked

Why "Hz Club"?

Because "Headphone Review Site #47" was taken. Also, Hz = Hertz = frequency = we're nerds.

Are you affiliated with any brands?

No. We buy our own gear. We owe nobody nothing.

What's with the design?

Bauhaus meets blog. If it was good enough for 1920s Germany, it's good enough for headphone reviews.

How do you test headphones?

Real music, real environments, real time. No artificial test tones unless we're really bored.
