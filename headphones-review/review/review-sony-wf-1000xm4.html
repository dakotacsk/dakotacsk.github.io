Sound

Well-balanced tuning that does nothing wrong. Slight warmth keeps things musical while maintaining clarity. Jack of all trades, master of most.

IEM build is premium with quality cables and housings. Fit depends on your ear shape but includes multiple tip sizes. Modern design that looks as good as it sounds.

Features

Bluetooth connectivity with reliable connection and decent battery life. Active noise cancelling that effectively reduces ambient noise.

What Works

  • Effective noise cancelling
  • Easy to drive

What Doesn't

Verdict

At 71%, the Sony WF-1000XM4 has its moments but faces stiff competition. There are better options available. For die-hard fans of the brand only.
