Frequently Asked Questions

How does the pilot program work?

Our pilot program offers 3 months of free access to the Nuggy platform. We work closely with you to optimize your pricing strategy and gather feedback to improve our product.

Which POS systems do you integrate with?

We're building integrations with major cannabis POS systems. During the pilot, we'll work with you to ensure compatibility with your specific system.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your pricing data is confidential and never shared with competitors.

What happens after the pilot?

Pilot participants get lifetime discounted pricing and priority access to new features. There's no obligation to continue if it's not a good fit.
