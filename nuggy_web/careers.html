Open Positions

UI/UX Design Intern

Part-time / Remote

We're looking for a talented UI/UX design intern to help us create an intuitive, beautiful experience for cannabis retailers. This is a perfect opportunity for someone looking to gain real-world experience in a fast-growing startup.

What You'll Do:

  • Design user interfaces for our web application
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
  • Conduct user research with dispensary owners and managers
  • Collaborate on our design system and brand guidelines
  • Help improve our landing page and marketing materials

What We're Looking For:

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed degree in Design, HCI, or related field
  • Portfolio demonstrating UI/UX design skills
  • Proficiency in Figma or similar design tools
  • Interest in data visualization and dashboard design
  • Enthusiasm for the cannabis industry is a plus!

What You'll Get:

  • Hands-on experience building a product from scratch
  • Direct mentorship from founders
  • Flexible hours (10-20 hours/week)
  • Remote work flexibility
  • NOTE: This is an unpaid internship with potential for equity.

Applications reviewed on a rolling basis
