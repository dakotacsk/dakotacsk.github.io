About Nuggy

Disrupting the cannabis industry one price at a time

Our Mission

We're on a mission to transform the cannabis industry's notoriously thin margins into sustainable, profitable businesses. Nuggy is your personal pricing buddy, designed specifically for dispensaries ready to embrace data-driven growth.

Why Cannabis Pricing Matters

The cannabis industry operates on some of the thinnest margins in retail. Between compliance costs, taxes, and fierce competition, dispensaries struggle to maintain profitability. That's where Nuggy comes in.

Our Approach

We believe pricing shouldn't be guesswork. Our AI-powered platform analyzes your sales data, monitors market trends, and optimizes prices in real-time to maximize your margins while keeping you competitive.
