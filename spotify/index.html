1. SLOW DANCING IN THE DARK - Joji:

Created by Japanese-American artist Joji, this song is often known as the best song to cry to by the internet. Joji used to be known under the name Filthy Frank on YouTube, often playing into stereotypes and making a joke of himself to gain popularity. I believe his songs will resonate with Oba's joker personality a lot, and he would love this newer, darker feel of Joji's music.


2. Nobody - Mitski:

Mitski Miyawaki was born Mitsuki Laycock on September 27, 1990, in Japan to a white American father and a Japanese mother. She is famous for her beautiful use of harmonies and expressive tones. This song perfectly embodies his feelings of not fitting in and being wanted by no community.


3. 我愛你 - Cody・Lee(李):

Made by a rising Japanese Indie-Rock Band, this song's title directly translates to "I love you" in English. Its cheerful vibe perfectly embodies the feeling of falling in love, and its lyrics also illustrate a beautiful love story that happens in the modern Japanese world. Since Oba always falls hard for whatever girl he is dating, this song will for sure resonate with him.


4. 怪物 - YOASOBI:

With a title that directly translates to "Monster", this song is used in a famous anime called Beastars that narrates a tragic story in a society of animals. This song is sung by the famous Japanese duo YOASOBI and is considered a classic by many who love J-Pop. Oba is no stranger to the alienation and discrimination the main character went through in this track.


5. 白日 - King Gnu:

Hakujitsu, written by a band named King Gnu, is a rock song expressing feelings of being in love with someone pure and knowing they are no match for them. The lyrics mention feeling like a sinner and wanting to be reborn. It perfectly encapsulates what Oba felt towards his last lover.
