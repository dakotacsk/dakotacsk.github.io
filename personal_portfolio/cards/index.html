Fun Fact #1
Anime Adaptation
Osamu Dazai is later adapted into a famous anime called Bungou Stray Dogs as a main character. He is a fan favorites and has a more comical approach to the suicidal ideation iconic to Osamu Dazai's writing.
Fun Fact #2
Manga Adaptation
No Longer Human was adapted into a manga by Junji Ito, a mangaka who is considered as a legend within the horror manga community.
Fun Fact #3
Best-Selling book
No Longer Human is the last completed work of Osamu Dazai, who ended his life shortly after publishing this book. Osamu Dazai soon proved himself to be one of the most influential writers in the history of Japanese literature, with No Longer Human still ranking as the second-best-selling novel ever in Japan. No Longer Human's impact is also undebatable, adapted to different formats such as films, anime, and manga.
Fun Fact #4
A Politician?
Dazai promised to stop associating himself with the ‘Japanese Communist Party’ on the condition that his allowance would be increased.
Fun Fact #5
Dazai's Mentor
He met his literary mentor, Ibuse Masuji, after writing a letter threatening to kill himself if Ibuse didn’t meet with him. Ibuse hated him but cannot deny that Dazai is an amazing writer.
