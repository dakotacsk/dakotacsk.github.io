Who am I?

Hello! My name is Dakota, and I created this website for fun. I grew up in Hong Kong and am currently studying in the US. I love mechanical keyboards, hi-fi equipment (portable hi-fis are my favorite), machine learning, playing percussion(jazz!!!), and social justice. I started my journey in computer science when I was six and discovered Scratch. I was fascinated by the logic behind code and how it created a virtual world that could interact with the user. Outside of school, I skate a lot (ice-skate, rollerblade, skateboarding, you name it, I've probably tried it). I also love a nice read and am a huge fan of animation and old Cantonese films.
