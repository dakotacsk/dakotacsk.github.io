Fun Fact #1
Anime Adaptation
Osamu Dazai is later adapted into a famous anime called Bungou Stray Dogs as a main character. He is a fan favorites and has a more comical approach to the suicidal ideation iconic to Osamu Dazai's writing.
Fun Fact #2
Manga Adaptation
No Longer Human was adapted into a manga by Junji Ito, a mangaka who is considered as a legend within the horror manga community.
Fun Fact #3
The Giant
Slow, steady and powerful, Giants are massive warriors that soak up huge amounts of damage. Show them a turret or cannon and you'll see their fury unleashed!
Fun Fact #4
The Goblin
These pesky little creatures only have eyes for one thing: LOOT! They are faster than a Spring Trap, and their hunger for resources is limitless.
