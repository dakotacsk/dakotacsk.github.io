Logo Proposals

A written explanation of “The Problem" that the logo is attempting to resolve.

The client would like to have a logo that can be put onto different materials/merch. They would also prefer to have the logo match their main color scheme of blue and for us to avoid yellow. It has to be easily identifiable as the logo of the community engagement office and should be modern-looking.

The key requests from your client (color, size, shape, concept)

The logo should be unique – no holding hands or hands holding hearts. It should also fit the blue color scheme, and be warm, welcoming, and modern looking. It is recommended for the logo to incorporate the non-sibi sun.
