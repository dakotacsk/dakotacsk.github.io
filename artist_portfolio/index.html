MY ART Artist Statement

Movie Poster

Dakota Chang is a Chinese artist living between Canada and Hong Kong. Visit dakotac.me to learn more.

Dakota has always been amazed by how humans communicate and perceive things. Her inspiration comes from subtle yet fascinating ways to convey messages. Minimalistic and discrete messages are littered through Dakota’s work and come together to create a cohesive and expressive final piece. She believes in the tiny details of an art piece and is not afraid to make it simple if it works. Dakota’s art surrounds a powerful and heartwarming story, but cannot be distinguished unless carefully investigated. Her work often start on a computer, and would later be transferred onto different mediums to embody its full essence.
